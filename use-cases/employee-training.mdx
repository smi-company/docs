<Tip>
  Whenever a manager explains a process for the second time, that's a signal to create a guide. Capture it once, share it forever.
</Tip>

## Share them everywhere

- **Slack** — team channels and automated onboarding flows
- **Notion, Confluence, or Guru** — embed inside knowledge base pages
- **LMS platforms** — add to training tracks and courses
- **Welcome emails** — include in new hire week-one sequences
- **HR platforms** — attach to onboarding checklists (Rippling, Workday)
- **Internal wiki** — a central library employees can search
