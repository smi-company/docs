## Share them everywhere

- **Welcome emails** — drop a guide link into the first sequence new customers receive
- **In-app checklists** — surface guides at the exact moment they're needed
- **CSM follow-ups** — attach after a call instead of re-typing instructions
- **Help centers** — build a self-serve library customers can revisit
- **Customer Slack channels** — answer in seconds with a visual walkthrough
- **QBR decks** — reference the exact guides driving adoption
