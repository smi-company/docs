</Tip>

## Share them everywhere

- **Release notes** — embed a guide at the top of every changelog
- **In-product banners** — surface at the moment users see the change
- **Knowledge base** — narrated walkthroughs alongside docs
- **Announcement emails** — the guide does the explaining
- **Slack launch threads** — internal walkthrough for the rollout
- **Sales enablement** — demo-ready content reps can share
- **Community posts** — power users discover and champion features
