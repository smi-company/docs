<Tip>
  Start with the five questions your team answers most often. Even a small library deflects a meaningful share of ticket volume.
</Tip>

## Share them everywhere

- **Help center articles** — embed guides where customers search
- **Live chat** — paste a link in Intercom, Zendesk, Crisp, or Gorgias
- **Email replies** — send a guide instead of a paragraph
- **In-app widgets** — surface guides where users get stuck
- **Ticket macros** — add to saved replies for one-click responses
- **Community portals** — publish where customers help each other
