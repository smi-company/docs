## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What do I need to use Guideless?" icon="rocket">
    All you need is a free Guideless account and the Chrome extension.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to record my screen or set up a microphone?" icon="mic-off">
    No. Guideless captures your workflow as steps and generates narration automatically with AI.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to make a guide?" icon="clock">
    Most users publish their first guide in under five minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Guideless work with my web app?" icon="globe">
    Yes. Guideless works with most web apps that run in Chrome, including SaaS tools, dashboards, and internal systems.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit a guide after recording?" icon="pencil-line">
    Yes. You can rename steps, edit narration, adjust zooms, add callouts, and remove unnecessary steps at any time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Capturing and editing guides

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit the AI narration?" icon="pencil">
    Yes. Edit the narration text for any step, then regenerate the voice-over to apply your changes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I reorder or delete steps?" icon="arrow-up-down">
    Yes. Reorder, rename, or delete steps at any time. You can also add callouts, annotations, and blur effects.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I miss a step during capture?" icon="redo-2">
    You can re-record the section, download an image and add it as the missing step manually in the editor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the voice after publishing?" icon="refresh-ccw">
    Yes. Change the voice and regenerate the narration at any time. Shared links and embeds update automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Sharing and embedding

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do viewers need an account to watch?" icon="user-x">
    No. Anyone with the link can watch immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I embed a guide?" icon="code">
    Anywhere iframe embeds are supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do my links break when I edit a guide?" icon="link">
    No. Existing links and embeds automatically show the latest version of your guide.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I download my guide as a video?" icon="download">
    Yes. Pro users can export guides as MP4 files for presentations, LMS platforms, or offline use.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I embed a guide and still update it later?" icon="rotate-ccw">
    Yes. Embedded guides update automatically whenever you publish changes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy and security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can see my guides?" icon="eye">
    Anyone with the link can view the guide. You control who receives the link and where it is shared.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is payment secure?" icon="lock">
    Yes. Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Guideless never stores your card details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I remove a guide later?" icon="file-x">
    Yes. You can edit, unpublish, or delete guides at any time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
