## Select a voice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the guide you want to narrate">
    Open the guide in the Guideless editor and navigate to the step-by-step walkthrough you want to convert into audio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Speech panel">
    Click the **Speech** icon in the left sidebar to open the narration panel. Guideless automatically generates a script based on your guide content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review or edit the narration">
    Review the generated narration script and make any adjustments if needed before creating the voice-over.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a voice">
    Click the voice selector icon in the Speech panel to open the voice library.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse and preview voices">
    Search voices by name or use the language and gender filters to find the voice that best matches your guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a voice">
    Click on a voice to preview it, then choose the one you want to use for your guide narration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the voice-over">
    Click **Generate voiceover** to create audio for your guide. Guideless converts the narration script into spoken audio and synchronizes it with the guide steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Preview a few voices before generating audio. Different voices vary in tone, pace, and style, so the best choice depends on your audience and guide type.
</Tip>

## Available voices

Choose from a growing library of AI voices across 40\+ languages to create guides for global teams and customers.

<Note>
  You can change the voice at any time. Regenerate the voice-over and all future viewers will hear the updated narration.
</Note>
