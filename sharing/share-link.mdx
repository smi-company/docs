## Share a guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your guide" icon="folder-open">
    Pick it from your Guideless dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Share" icon="share-2">
    The share panel opens with your link ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy" icon="copy">
    One click copies the URL to your clipboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste anywhere" icon="clipboard-paste">
    Send, post, embed, or pin it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Flexible access control

Choose who can access your guide. Keep it private while creating content or share it instantly with anyone using a public link.
