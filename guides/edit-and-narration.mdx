## Edit the narration script

Guideless automatically generates a narration script from your recorded steps. No writing required.

- Click any line to edit it directly
- Rewrite as little or as much as you want
- Changes are applied the next time you generate the voiceover

<Frame>
  ![AI Written Narration 2](/images/AI-written-narration-2.jpg)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Keep narration concise. One action per sentence. Short lines are easier to follow when spoken aloud.
</Tip>

<Note>
  After editing, click **Generate voiceover** to refresh the audio. The previous take is replaced automatically.
</Note>

## Pick a voice

Open the **Speech** panel to preview and apply AI voices in seconds. Guideless supports 40\+ languages, so you can localize guides without re-recording your screen capture.

<Info>
  You can switch the voice and regenerate at any time, even after the guide is live. Viewers always hear the latest version.
</Info>

## Polish the guide experience

Before publishing, refine both the visual and narration experience of your guide.

- Adjust **Smart Zooms** to focus on important actions
- Choose **cursor** styles that match different workflows
- Resize the **cursor** for better visibility
- Review and refine the narration before generating audio
