## What you can customize

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Backgrounds" icon="image">
    Choose backgrounds that make your guides look polished and professional.

  <Card title="Colors" icon="paintbrush">
    Match colors and accents to create a consistent look across your guides.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Cursor & Callouts" icon="highlighter">

## Set it up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a guide" icon="folder-open">
    Select any guide from your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open branding settings" icon="settings">
    Use the left sidebar to pick backgrounds, borders, or cursor styles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the look" icon="paintbrush">
    Adjust colors, shapes, and visual settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview changes" icon="presentation">
    See updates instantly in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and continue" icon="check">
    Your guide is updated automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Consistent branding is recognition. Whether the viewer hits an internal SOP or a public help article, the experience feels like one product.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Branding works on Free and Pro. The Free plan adds a small Guideless watermark. Upgrade to remove it. See [pricing](/plans).
</Note>
