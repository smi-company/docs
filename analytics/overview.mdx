## View analytics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your guide" icon="folder-open">
    Open the guide you want to analyze.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Insights" icon="chart-spline">
    Open the **Insights** tab in the guide editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review performance" icon="eye">
    See how viewers engage with your guide from start to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Turn insights into better guides

Use analytics trends to improve guide clarity and completion rates.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Early drop-off" icon="chart-bar-decreasing">
    Viewers leave within the first few steps. Review the introduction and look for opportunities to get to the first action sooner.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Mid-guide drop-off" icon="git-branch">
    Viewers stop watching partway through the guide. Review the surrounding steps and look for opportunities to improve clarity or pacing.
  </Card>

  <Card title="High replay count" icon="rotate-ccw">
    Viewers return to the same step multiple times. Consider adding a callout or simplifying the explanation.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Strong completion" icon="check-check">
    Most viewers make it to the end of the guide. Consider using it as a template for future guides.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
